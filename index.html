Research Interests

I'm interested in human motion in general, with an emphasis on the dynamic simulation and control of virtual human characters. My Ph.D thesis presents a motion capture dimension reduction framework that integrates easily with existing kinematic and dynamic animation systems. The resulting data-driven, reduced-dimension kinematics produce quality animations at a smaller computational cost. Some example results can be seen below:


Lately I've been shifting towards humanoid robot control and soft tissue simulation.
